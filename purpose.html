Funnle

/purpose

Funnle.io is based on these following principles:

  1. The web is awesome.
  2. You can spend days browsing it and find the most amazing things.
  3. It's hard to find time doing just that.

There got to be a better way!

Oh wait, there is. We just made it. And you are reading it's /purpose.

Funnl.io captures that browsing awesomeness and delivers it too you – when you have the time. All you have to do is to share your favourite links (which we all do anyways).

We'll consider this site a moderate success if some people actually does share and an even bigger succes if we hear stories of great links recieved.

Ultimately, we’re just happy too try and give something (meaningful) back to the web.

Such humble.
